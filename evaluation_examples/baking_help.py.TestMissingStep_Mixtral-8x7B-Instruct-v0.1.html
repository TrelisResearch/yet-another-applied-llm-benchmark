But they're terrible! Why? List the main reason.

Output

The main reason your pancakes might be terrible is that you added the dry ingredients before the wet ingredients. This can result in a lumpy batter and tough pancakes. It's generally recommended to mix the wet ingredients together separately, then add them to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Overmixing can also result in tough pancakes, so it's important to mix the batter only until the ingredients are combined. Additionally, the type and quality of the ingredients used, as well as the temperature of the pan, can also affect the outcome of the pancakes.
